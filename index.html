🛠️ AI Tools Used

This project uses AI tools to improve workplace productivity:

  • ChatGPT - brainstorming and writing assistance
  • Google Gemini - research and AI exploration
  • Notion AI - productivity support

💡 Prompt Engineering

Clear prompts help AI generate better results.

  • Create a professional workplace email.
  • Summarize meeting notes into key points.
  • Create a daily task plan.
  • Explain research topics clearly.

🔒 Responsible AI

This assistant demonstrates how AI can improve workplace productivity. All AI-generated content should be reviewed by a human before being used in professional or business environments.
